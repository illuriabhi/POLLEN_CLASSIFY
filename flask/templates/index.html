Pollen Grain Prediction
We assist researchers and scientists in the field of botany and plant sciences by providing interface for users to upload images of pollen grains and give predictions on the type of pollen present in the image

ABOUT US

Pollen Grain Classification is an AI-powered image prediction platform that helps scientists, educators, and ecologists identify pollen types using deep learning models. Built with Flask and TensorFlow, this web app bridges environmental biology and machine learning to streamline research and data gathering.

The project is part of a team innovation initiative focused on delivering real-world applications of computer vision for scientific analysis. Users can upload microscope-captured pollen images and receive instant species-level predictions based on our trained model.
